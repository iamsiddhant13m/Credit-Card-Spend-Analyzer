# Credit Card Spend Analyzer 📊

**End-to-End Business Analytics Dashboard**  
**Power BI | SQL | Python**

A complete credit card transaction analysis project that uncovers revenue drivers, customer behaviour, risk patterns, and strategic insights for Banking & FinTech companies.

![Dashboard Overview](dashboard/overview.png)

---

### 🎯 Project Objective
Analyzed **100,000 credit card transactions** from **10,000 customers** over 2 years to help business stakeholders make data-driven decisions on revenue growth, customer segmentation, and risk management.

### 📈 Key Business Insights
- **Total GMV**: ₹69.74 Crore  
- **Total Revenue**: ₹70.47 Crore  
- **Highest Spending Tier**: Platinum  
- **Top Spending Category**: Shopping  
- **Peak Spending Month**: October 2024  
- **Highest Default Rate**: Silver tier (3.15%)  
- **Top Revenue City**: Mumbai  
- **Highest Spending Occupation**: Salaried

### 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Python** — Data generation & Exploratory Data Analysis (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- **MySQL** — Database design and complex analytical queries
- **Power BI** — Interactive dashboard with slicers and professional visuals
- **SQL** — 10+ business-focused analytical queries

### 📊 Interactive Dashboard Features
- **Executive Overview** — KPIs, Revenue trends, Category & Channel mix
- **Customer Segmentation** — Spend by Occupation, City, Card Tier + Interactive Slicers
- **Risk & Insights** — Default rate analysis, Top 5 cities, Monthly trends

**Live Slicers**: Filter dynamically by City, Card Tier, and Occupation

![Customer Segmentation Page](dashboard/segmentation.png)

### 🧠 Strategic Recommendations
- Focus premium marketing and offers on **Platinum & Gold** customers in Mumbai and Delhi.
- Strengthen credit risk monitoring for **Silver** tier customers due to higher default rate.
- Target **Salaried** professionals for cross-selling and upselling opportunities.
- Plan aggressive festive campaigns in **October** to maximize revenue.
